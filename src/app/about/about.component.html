What is CLIPZ ?

CLIPZ is an online video streaming application where you can upload your gaming highlights and share them with the world!

But it doesn't stop here. You can watch other the highlights of other gamers as well. You can share your favorite highlights with your friends online.

CLIPZ uses the latest and greatest WebAssembly technology under the hood. No need to worry about laggy videos.

By using ffmpeg for video processing, there is no room for laggy user experience. Feel the speed. There is no reason to worry about slow video uploads.
